Lisbon

My trip to Lisbon, Portugal.

July, 2016

Lisbon is Portugal's capital and largest city. Lisbon is a very hilly coastal city with lots of beautiful vistas!

Lisbon offers a rich and varied history, a buzzing nightlife and is blessed with a glorious year-round climate. You can read more about Lisbon here.

City Attractions

  • Belem Tower
  • Alfama district
  • Castelo de S. Jorge
  • Jeronimos Monastery
