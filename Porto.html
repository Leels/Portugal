Porto

My trip to Porto, Portugal.

July, 2016

Porto is a coastal city in northwest Portugal. It is Portugal's second largest city and is know for its stately bridges and port wine production.

Porto is one of Europe's oldest cities. You an read about Porto's history here.

City Attractions

  • Luis Bridge
  • Livraria Lello (ornate bookshop said to be the inspiration for Hogwart's library)
  • Clerigos Church
  • Port Wine Cellars
